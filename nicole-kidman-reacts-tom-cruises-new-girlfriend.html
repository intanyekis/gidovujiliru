Nicole Kidman Reacts To Tom Cruises New Girlfriend, Thinks Relationship Is Bizarre

Highlights

  • Nicole Kidman believes Tom Cruise's new romance is staged for the public and thinks it won't last.
  • Kidman thinks Cruise is undateable and believes his ego and bizarre lifestyle will eventually end the relationship.
  • Tom's girlfriend's ex-husband warned him about her motivations for being in a relationship with him.

It’s been over two decades since Tom Cruise and Nicole Kidman ended their marriage. But it appears she has some thoughts on the Mission: Impossible actor’s new girlfriend, Elsina Khayrova.

Speaking to National Enquirer, a source claims that Kidman believes Cruise’s new romance is completely “staged” for the public. "It all seems staged and bizarre to her and makes her even more thankful for her own marriage,” the insider shared.

Tom and Nicole were married from 1990 to 2001, making their union last for nearly 11 years. During their time together, they adopted two children, Isabella and Connor, who are now adults. Nicole has said the kids opted to live with their dad following their separation.

Several years after the divorce, Kidman married country singer Keith Urban in 2006. The couple has two biological daughters, Sunday, born in 2008, and Faith, born in 2010.

For his part, Tom went on to marry again to Katie Holmes in 2006. The couple welcomed their daughter, Suri Cruise, the same year, though their relationship ended in divorce in 2012.

Nicole Believes Tom’s Romance Is Staged And Won’t Last

It’s been speculated that Tom’s connection to Scientology contributed to both of his divorces. There have also been rumors throughout the years that the Church has had a hand in Tom’s romantic life, and Nicole’s reaction to his new partner seems to suggest she believes their relationship is merely set-up for the public’s benefit.

"Tom's shown for years he's undateable and if he thinks he's going to solve that problem by throwing money at his high-maintenance sweetie, then good luck with that,” the insider continued.

"Nicole knows what a ham Tom is," they added. "He can pour on the charm and hire out an entire restaurant for one meal, but there's no substance there."

Apparently, Nicole doesn’t think Tom’s new relationship will stand the test of time and that they’ll break up eventually.

"Somewhere down the line, Tom's going to get bored and distracted by other things and Elsina's going to kick him to the curb,” the source explained. "It's not like he can't afford to lavish this girl with the best of the best, but Nicole believes Tom's ego and bizarre lifestyle will eventually burn this romance out like all the others!"

Tom’s Girlfriend’s Ex-Husband Warned Him About Her Motivations

While Tom may be romancing Elsina to prove something to the public – she’s the first woman he’s been publicly linked to in years – she may also have her own motivations for getting in a relationship with the famous actor. For instance, Elsina’s ex-husband, Russian oligarch Dmitry Tsetkov, issued a public warning to Cruise, saying she has expensive taste and chooses her partners based on the type of luxurious lifestyle they can offer her.

For now, Cruise hasn’t responded to the controversy surrounding his dating life, but reports continue to say the actor is serious about his newly publicized relationship.
