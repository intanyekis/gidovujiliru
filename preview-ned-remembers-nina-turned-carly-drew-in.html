Ned Remembers Nina Turned Carly and Drew In

In General Hospital’s preview for the week of November 13 – 17, the truth about Nina could come out any day now. Read on for the scoop and watch the teaser video.

Last week, in the aftermath of Anna shooting Charlotte, Dante began to piece together that it was Charlotte who was stalking his friend. Anna believed Charlotte was being used by a former WSB agent out to get her, and Valentin was finally forced to confirm it had been Charlotte all along.

Coming up, at the station, Anna informs Dante and Jordan that Charlotte has been stalking her for months. What does this mean for Charlotte’s future? Over at Valentin’s place, Laura informs him and Nina that Charlotte believes Anna wanted to kill her. Will they be able to undo Victor’s brainwashing of the girl?

Michael’s known since his father’s wedding that Nina was the one who turned his mother and Drew in to the SEC. Rather than expose the secret, he’s been using it to keep her away from his family. Unfortunately for Michael, Nina and Willow have grown closer. Willow tells Michael that Nina’s changed, but Michael tells her that she’s wrong. She asks, “Wrong how?” Will Michael tell her the truth?

One thing is for sure, if Michael doesn’t expose Nina, someone else could! In bed with Liv, Ned, having regained his memory, remembers something very important. And finally, Lois appears shocked.
