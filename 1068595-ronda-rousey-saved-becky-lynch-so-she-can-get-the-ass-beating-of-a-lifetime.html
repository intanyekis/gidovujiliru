Ronda Rousey Saved Becky Lynch So She Can Get The Ass Beating Of A Lifetime

Ronda Rousey’s latest video blog is now live and it goes over both WWE Fastlane and last week’s Monday Night RAW in Pittsburgh. During the video, Rousey discusses why she saved Becky Lynch when it looked like she was going to lose to Charlotte Flair.

“I saw that Charlotte was putting the Figure Eight on Becky and nobody has ever gotten out of that,” said Rousey. “Becky received the first actual beating she’s ever gotten in her life [on the RAW before Fastlane], so I wouldn’t put it past her to throw the match so that she could save face. She’d say something like, ‘Oh, my knee was hurt. Vince is mean. It’s not my fault,’ but I saved her fragile face for a reason. I wanted her to be at WrestleMania and get the first true head-to-toe ass beating of a lifetime.”
