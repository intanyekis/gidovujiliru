Sushmita Sen is an Indian actress and model who won Miss Universe 1994 at the age of 18, becoming the first Indian to achieve the feat. Prior to that, she was crowned as Femina Miss India in 1994. Since then, she has worked in the Indian entertainment industry and has been a part of a variety of films and shows such as Maine Pyaar Kyun Kiya?, Biwi No.1, Main Hoon Na, Mudhalvan, Kyo Kii… Main Jhuth Nahin Bolta, Aankhen, Sirf Tum, Ram Gopal Varma Ki Aag, Zindaggi Rocks, Kisna: The Warrior Poet, Do Knot Disturb, F.A.L.T.U, Karma Aur Holi, No Problem, Vaastu Shastra, Tumko Na Bhool Paayenge, Paisa Vasool, Main Aisa Hi Hoon, Bas Itna Sa Khwaab Hai, Nayak, Aaghaaz, and Fiza.

    Sushmita Sen Facts

  • She is the first Indian woman to win the Miss Universe Contest in 1994.
  • Often, she is known as the most stylish and glamorous lady in India.
  • In 2000, when Sushmita was 25, she made history by adopting a baby girl, named Renée. After approximately 10 years, she adopted another baby girl (of three months) in January 2010 and she was named, Alisah.
  • She has been diagnosed with Addison’s disease and requires lifelong steroid drugs to manage the illness.
  • Just after the release of her debut film, Dastak in 1996, she was believed to be too tall and criticized for being too western for Hindi movies.
  • Sen likes to write poetry.
  • As of 2013, her biggest hit movie has been “Main Hoon Na” (2004) in which she was the love interest of Shahrukh Khan.
  • She has received various awards for her performances in several movies in 2000, 2001, 2003, and 2006.
  • In 2020, she started playing the character named Aarya Sareen, an independent woman who joins the mafia gang in order to seek revenge on her husband’s murder, in the Disney+ Hotstar Hindi-language crime drama series, Aarya, in which she co-starred alongside the likes of Chandrachur Singh, Namit Das, Ankur Bhatia, and Vikas Kumar.
  • For her performance in Aarya, she won the 2020 Filmfare OTT Award for “Best Actor in a Drama Series (Female)”.
  • She has also worked with actors like Amitabh Bachchan, Akshay Kumar, Paresh Rawal, Ajay Devgan, Salman Khan, and Katrina Kaif.
  • In 2022, Sushmita revealed that she came close to getting married thrice in her life. However, she chose not to get married every time.
  • In 2022, Sushmita’s brother Rajeev and his wife Charu got separated and filed for divorce (which they later called off). Rajeev gave the reason that he was not aware of Charu’s first marriage. However, Charu said that he was already aware of this fact. They also had a daughter together. Later, in October 2022, they were once again going for a divorce. This time, Charu found out that he was cheating on her when she was pregnant.
  • Sushmita’s Twitter account was verified in January 2011. She had thanked Lalit Kumar Modi for the same on Twitter. This tweet went viral in July 2022 after Modi announced that they had started dating.
  • In February 2023, Sushmita underwent an angioplasty (and a stent was placed in her body) as she had suffered a heart attack. There was 95% blockage in her main artery.
  • In August 2023, Sushmita revealed that director Farah Khan had apologized to her after the completion of the film Main Hoon Na because Sushmita was barely there in the film after the final editing was done.
