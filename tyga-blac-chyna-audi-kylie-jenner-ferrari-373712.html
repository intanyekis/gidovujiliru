Tyga Blasts Blac Chyna's New Whip, Brags About Buying Kylie Jenner A Ferrari

The latest rumor to hit the mill regarding Tyga and Kylie Jenner stems from his lavish gift for his reality star girlfriend on her recent 18th birthday. After buzz surrounded a certain Mercedes-Benz G-Wagen that may or may not have belonged to the rapper’s ex Blac Chyna, Tyga outdid himself with a brand new Ferrari for Jenner. Now, the Internet is buzzing again, as sites are reporting that Jenner leased the Ferrari herself. In response to the alleged lease, Tyga took to Instagram with a selfie and a brief caption.

“When the hating don’t work they start telling lies. #WhereYoFerarriTho?” he wrote.
