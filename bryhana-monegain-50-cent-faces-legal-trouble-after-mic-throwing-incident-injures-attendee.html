Bryhana Monegain: 50 Cent faces legal trouble after Mic-throwing incident injures attendee

50 Cent is making headlines after a recent performance at the Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles took a chaotic turn. During his set, the rapper threw his microphone into the audience, unintentionally injuring one of his fans, Bryhana Monegain, who was in attendance and enjoying the show. Monegain, known for her appearances on the hip-hop station Power 106, sustained injuries when the microphone reportedly struck her in the head.

Interestingly, this incident comes on the heels of a similar occurrence involving Cardi B, who threw her microphone at an audience member during her performance in Las Vegas after being targeted with a liquid projectile.

Bryhana Monegain is a familiar face on Power 106, known for her appearances in stage plays and various commercials. Her IMDb profile reveals her academic background, having graduated from the University of Missouri with a degree in mass media communication and a minor in theater. She furthered her studies in acting in California and has had a successful career working at several radio stations over the years.

Reports indicate that during the event, organizers provided 50 Cent with malfunctioning microphones, which seemingly frustrated the rapper and led to his mic-throwing incident. While TikTok videos captured the incident, they did not provide a clear view of where the microphone landed.

According to Page Six, Bryhana Monegain sustained injuries to her forehead, which required stitches, along with scratches on her hand. She has reportedly filed a complaint, and 50 Cent is currently a suspect in a criminal felony battery investigation.
