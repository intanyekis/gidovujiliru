Diana Valenzuela obituary: Young girl passes away in car accident in Longview, TX

Longview, Texas – The Longview community is reeling from the devastating loss of Diana Valenzuela, who tragically passed away on Monday, September 18, 2023, in a fatal motor vehicle accident that left her with severe injuries, ultimately leading to her untimely demise.

The news of Diana’s passing has sent shockwaves through her family and friends, leaving them grappling with the harsh reality of her absence. Diana Monsivais, a dear friend from Madrid, Spain, expressed her grief on Facebook, writing, “when I just literally saw you this weekend and we planned to hang out again.. like what??? Not you!! Who’s going to call me ‘mi flaca’ only you!!!! Those tight hugs you’d always give me, made me feel all warm an shit… wtf!!…this is not real, why do good ppl leave us.”

Diana Valenzuela’s sudden departure has left a void in the lives of those who knew her. Her warm embrace and endearing nickname, “mi flaca,” were cherished by all who had the privilege of experiencing her affectionate friendship.

As the community mourns this heartbreaking loss, our thoughts and condolences go out to Diana Valenzuela’s family and friends, who must navigate through this difficult time while remembering the warmth and love she shared with everyone she met.
