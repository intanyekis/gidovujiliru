Bernhard Goetz Wikipedia: What Did He Do?

Bernhard Goetz does have a mention under the Wikipedia page, 1984 New York City Subway Shooting. 

An electrician at the time, Goetz had a model 38 handgun, a weapon he possessed illegally. He came into the possession of the weapon after his apartment was robbed a few weeks ago.

Moreover, he was traveling on a subway train in New York when a black man and three friends approached him. One of them showed a screwdriver and asked him for money.

Goetz, in return, pulled his handgun and shot each of them. He shot one twice after saying, “You seem to be all right, here’s another.”
