This Is What Chris Kattan's Life And Net Worth Look Like Today

Don't touch the mango! Chris Kattan is best known for his time on Saturday Night Live (SNL) and his acting career, which includes A Night at the Roxbury, The Middle and Bunnicula.SNL saw him take on many roles including Mango, The Roxbury Guys, Mr. Peepers and more.

The actor and comedian is currently competing as a house guest on the third season of Celebrity Big Brother. He is not so much is in the spotlight anymore but is continuing to act and perform comedy as much as he can.

Kattan sustained an injury on the set of SNL that set his career and life back a bit, but he doesn't let that stop him. He told the audience and judges about his injury during his time on Dancing With The Stars.

So, what exactly is the 51-year-old up to in 2022? This is what Chris Kattan's life and net worth look like today.

7 Chris Kattan's Neck Injury

Chris Kattan is still suffering from an injury he received more than 20 years ago on the set of SNL. In his memoir, "Baby, Don't Hurt Me: Stories And Scars From Saturday Night Live," he recounted that he broke his neck and injured his spinal cord during a May 2001 episode of the show. The comedian threw himself backwards on a chair and hit his head hard on the stage, later resulting in five surgeries. As for today, he still suffers from lingering neck pain and stumbling. He still takes medicine to this day to help the pain.

6 Chris Kattan's Girlfriend

Kattan has been linked to a number of actresses and high-profile women over the years. Since leaving the Celebrity Big Brother house, Kattan has posted a few times with his current girlfriend, Maria Libri. She is a former on- air personality for Fox stations WRSP and WCCU, who currently lives in Springfield, IL. It is unclear how long they have been together, but Kattan was formerly linked to model Cheyenne Gordon in 2021.

5 'Hey Kattan!'

Launched in late 2021, 'Hey Kattan!' is a sketch show on his YouTube channel. It is a weekly show that has him performing sketches, showing archival footage and comedy content. The channel only has 5.8k followers, but the videos earn a lot more views than that. This is one way for him to earn money and also stay active and involved in the comedy community.

4 Chris Kattan's Current Film Roles

In December 2021, Chris Kattan attended the premiere for his film, Famous, which premiered on Amazon Prime Video and in theaters. According to IMDb, the film's summary is, "Driven by a devastating need to expose the truth, celebrity Jason Mast pushes the boundaries of friendship to reveal the true cost of fame. What appears to be a night of celebration turns out to be an intricate plan to confront his past." Kattan plays a character called Lawrence Nichols. He also starred in a movie called 40-Love, which came out in November 2021.

3 Chris Kattan Still Performs Stand-Up Comedy

A few months ago, Kattan, along with other SNL alum, Tim Meadows, Finesse Mitchell and Jon Lovitz, performed across the country in the "Veterans of SNL" tour. Sometimes one of them would be swapped out with another alum, such as Darrell Hammond. If you are a lover of the old school SNL, then this was surely a show for you to attend. Laughs and memories were made. He also performed in solo shows as well.

2 Chris Kattan On 'Celebrity Big Brother'

Chris Kattan is one of 11 house guests on this season's Celebrity Big Brother. Being one of the older celebrities in the house might put him at a bit of a disadvantage, but his humor and sweet disposition gives him some props in the game. He is lying low, and despite not being in any alliances, the rest of the house seems to like him. As of writing this article, Kattan is still in the game.

1 Chris Kattan's Net Worth

According to Celebrity Net Worth, as of February 2022, Kattan's net worth is $6 million. Some may find it shocking that his net worth is on the lower side because of the success he has had with SNL, acting and comedy. However, having to pay for multiple surgeries and being arrested for a DUI and then putting up bail might have hindered his earnings a bit.
