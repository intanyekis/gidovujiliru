Here's What Amanda Bynes Has Been Up To In 2021

Its been over 10 years since Amanda Bynes has been in a movie. Her last film was Easy A in 2010 and then her life turned for the worse. She was suffering from mental illness and substance abuse.She was a 2000s icon and was on almost every TV during that time. But since her breakdown in 2012, she has not acted. In fact, after her parents stepped in and helped her get sober, Bynes enrolled in school to study fashion. She had hoped to make her own fashion line. However, nothing has come to fruition yet.Bynes then went into rehab and met someone there. Then, Amanda joined Instagram and told her followers some good news. She later claimed she was engaged and pregnant but the pregnancy turned out to be just a rumor. Since then, she has been pretty calm.It may only be halfway through the year, but here's what Amanda Bynes has been up to in 2021.

8 Celebrated Her 35th Birthday

It's hard to believe Amanda Bynes is 35, because we've seen her as a child and young adult in movies, but when a decade goes by, this is what happens. On April 3, she celebrated her 35th birthday with family and friends, according to her lawyer David A. Esquibias. He talked to PEOPLE and told the magazine that the former actress is "doing great." Not much else is known about the celebration.

7 Released A Rap Song

In the beginning of January, Bynes added another thing to her resume- rapping. She posted a short snippet of the song called "Diamonds" to her Instagram and YouTube channel. In the one-minute clip, she is heard to be rapping with rapper Precise, who is believed to be her fiancé Paul Michael. He also posted a video of him singing the song on his TikTok. She seems to be singing on the chorus, "Diamonds, diamonds, diamonds / On my neck / On my wrist / Diamonds, diamonds, diamonds." Although she released the teaser the song was never put on streaming platforms.

6 Enjoying Soul Cycle Classes

According to her lawyer, he told PEOPLE that "she lives by the beach, attends school and is enjoying mediation and Soul Cycle classes." And she was looking forward to spending her birthday and time with her family and friends. Bynes is in great spirits and seems to be in a better place mentally and physically than she has in the past few years. Although she has not gotten back into acting yet, she is focusing on herself.

5 Still Attending School

In 2019, Bynes posted a graduation photo on Twitter where she earned her Associates Degree from California's Fashion Institute of Design and Merchandising. Now, she is continuing her education to get her Bachelors Degree in Fashion. She wanted to launch an online clothing brand and website, which hasn't yet come to fruition. Now, Bynes wants to add to her line.

4 Might Start Her Own Clothing Or Perfume Line

In October 2020, Amanda Bynes attorney, Esquibias said, “Amanda is very entrepreneurial. She is investigating fragrances. She is now considering perfume in addition to a clothing line. But, don't get too excited. She is still a student at FIDM earning her degree."

In a recent interview, according to Page Six, Esquibias explained, “She’s exploring what options remain open to her while she continues her degree at FIDM. Clothes, perfumes, she’s going to investigate those areas further.”

3 She Is Still Engaged

Despite Bynes posting a sonogram on Instagram last year, she is not pregnant, but she did get engaged to Paul Michael on Valentine's Day 2020, who she met at a sober living home in 2019. The couple doesn't live together, and the pandemic was hard on them. They even broke off their engagement for a few months, but are now back together. He is supposedly a great influence on her and is an advocate for her sobriety and is a nice person, according to unnamed sources.

2 She Is Under A Conservatorship

Like Britney Spears, Amanda Bynes has been under a conservatorship for many years. Her mother currently has complete control over her affairs and finances, and she has since Bynes started having legal problems, which landed her in a mental health facility. That means that Amanda is unable to marry her fiancé, unless she has her mother's permission. Her mother has refused her to marry Paul Michael, saying she doesn't believe Amanda is in any capacity to make decisions about her care or treatment. They want her to remain in a controlled environment. However, Bynes has tried to fight it in court but has not won.

1 Wiped Her Instagram Account

Bynes has lashed out on Twitter before at many celebrities but has since deleted the posts. Although she has not posted anything since 2019, her Twitter is still up. Bynes made an official Instagram account last year, but wipes it every few months. She used to update her 25k followers with her life and selfies she would take, showing them that she got a face tattoo and nose ring. But currently, the account is empty. No one knows if that means she spiraled again, or if she just wants privacy and posted those when she was not in a good place.
