Intimate Wedding With Husband Eduardo Saverin

How Elaine Andriejanssen's Husband Made His Fortunes?

Her husband, Eduardo along with Mark Zuckerberg and others co-founded Facebook which was launched in February 2004. As of 2018, he owns 2% of Facebook’s outstanding shares -- approximately 53 million shares.

In 2011, he renounced U.S. citizenship and as a result, avoided $700 million in capital gains taxes. However, the billionaire has denied the allegation of tax fraud citing his ‘interest in working and living in Singapore.’

One of his portfolio companies, Jumio, a Palo Alto-based identity verification service provider is estimated to generate $10 billion revenue in 2018. By 2022, Jumio is expected to double its current income to $20 billion.
