Fans Are Concerned With Jamie Lynn Spears' TBT Photo Going Barefoot At A Gas Station

Zoey 101 actress Jamie Lynn Spears is no stranger to being herself, and that has stayed true as she has lived in Mississippi for most of her life. Being a country girl her life, she is no stranger to doing some things that seem bizarre to those that say, live in the northern states. One of those things is apparently going barefoot at a gas station. Fans reacted in mixed response to Jamie's photo before the pandemic occurred, confused and concerned.

Similar to how things are different in each country, the same can be said for specific regions in the United States. While the idea of going barefoot anywhere sounds unsanitary, as long as Jamie takes care of her feet, there's no harm done unless a law regarding wearing shoes is extremely mandatory.
