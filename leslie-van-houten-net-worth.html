Leslie Van Houten Wikipedia

Leslie Van Houten Wikipedia: - Leslie is a convicted murderer from the United States and a former Manson Family member. She went under several aliases while she was a member of Manson's cult, including Louella Alexandria, Leslie Marie Sankston, Linda Sue Owens, and Lulu. In connection with the slayings of Leno and Rosemary LaBianca in 1969, Van Houten was detained and accused. She was found guilty and given the death sentence. However, the death penalty was later declared to be illegal in People v. Anderson by the California Supreme Court, leading to her sentence being commuted to life in prison in 1972. A 1976 judgment by an appellate court overturned her conviction and allowed her a new trial. A deadlocked jury and a mistrial resulted from her second trial. She was found guilty of two charges of murder and one count of conspiracy at her third trial in 1978 and given a seven- to life sentence. In 2023, she was granted release.
