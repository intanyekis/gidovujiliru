Chrissy Teigen Cleverly Covers Nipple After Instagram Deletes Nude Pic

HAIR @jrugg8

A photo posted by @chrissyteigen on Jun 30, 2015 at 2:38pm PDT

Hairspray is handy in more ways than one! Chrissy Teigen found a loophole in Instagram’s no-nudity policy. After the site took down several of her racy snapshots, the model, 29, decided to post yet another topless photo — without showing her nipple!

Teigen, who’s married to John Legend, took a selfie while sitting in a makeup chair, and was surrounded by eye shadows and brushes galore. But it was a bottle of hairspray she used to strategically cover a part of her breast. “HAIR @jrugg8,” she captioned the pic.

One day earlier, Teigen posted a topless photo from a W magazine photo shoot, only to have it removed by the app. Undeterred, she continued to share it each time Instagram deleted it. Her response? “The nipple has been temporarily silenced but she will be back, oh yes, she will be back,” she teased via Twitter.
