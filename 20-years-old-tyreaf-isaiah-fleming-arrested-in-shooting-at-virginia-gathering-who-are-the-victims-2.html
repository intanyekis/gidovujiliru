Who Are The Victims Of Shooting At Virginia Gathering?

The shooting victimized several people carried out by an individual at a Virginia gathering.

According to witnesses, “one individual or individuals shot multiple rounds into a crowd at an outdoor Party” at around 2:20 in the 1500 block of Devon Lane, eight victims, ages 18 to 27, were taken to the hospital.

According to authorities, three injured were moved to UVA Medical Center, while three others received treatment at Sentara RMH Medical Center. Officials described all of their injuries as not life-threatening.

Investigators believe the occurrence is “contained,” and there is no “risk to the larger community.”

None of the victims, according to University officials, are students. At approximately three in the morning, the school used the LiveSafe app to send a shelter-in-place message.
