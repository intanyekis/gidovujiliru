How Many Tattoos Did Pete Davidson Get For Kim Kardashian And Ariana Grande?

Pete Davidson has over 70 different tattoos, many of which are directly inspired by his past relationships. Being celebrated as one of the most eligible bachelors in Hollywood is no small feat, and Pete Davidson has a reputation for getting tattoos that represent his past lovers.

Unfortunately for Pete, this usually ends up with him getting them removed after the relationship ends, such was also the case when Pete Davidson and Kim Kardashian ended their highly publicized relationship.

From his most recent fling with Emily Ratajkowski to his five-month relationship with Ariana Grande that resulted in a broken engagement, his relationships are always an interesting source of celebrity gossip. Here's how many tattoos Pete Davidson got for his superstar exes Kim Kardashian and Ariana Grande...

These Are All The Tattoos That Pete Davidson Got In Honor Of Kim Kardashian

Kim Kardashian and Pete Davidson dated for a total of 9 months, with their relationship consistently being a hot topic throughout the year of 2022. Kim Kardashian was very vocal about her love and admiration for Pete Davidson.

She gushed about her then-boyfriend in an episode of The Kardashians, saying: "Pete has got to be the best human being I have ever met. He always wants the best for people, he can handle anything, and he always does it with grace. He is really thoughtful, humble and so genuine. I would say the perfect word to describe Pete is genuine."

While Pete wasn't as vocal as Kim when it came to opening up about their relationship to the public, he showed his love for Kim in other ways, the Saturday Night Live star got a total of three tattoos and one branding dedicated to the reality TV star.

The first tattoo Pete Davidson got for Kim Kardashian was located on his collarbone, with the words "my girl is a lawyer", a subtle brag about Kardashian's decision to follow in her father's footsteps and become a lawyer. Kim noted that this tattoo was her favorite, even posting a photo of the tattoo to her Instagram Stories.

On his other collarbone, he had another tattoo with the words "Jasmine and Aladdin", Pete Davidson got these particular words tattooed as an homage to their first kiss in their now famous Aladdin-themed Saturday Night Live skit. This moment was clearly very special for the pair, as the two first connected on the set on Saturday Night Live.

Above the Jasmine and Aladdin tattoo, he also got the letters "KNSCP", which many eagle-eyed fans believe to be a reference to the names of Kim Kardashian and her four children (Kim, North, Saint, Chicago, and Psalm). This particular tattoo was infamously mentioned in an Instagram post by Kanye West that read: "Ask Pete how those tattoos of my kids doing in the trauma unit".

Additionally, Kim Kardashian revealed in an Ellen Show interview that Pete Davidson branded himself with her first name, speaking to Ellen DeGeneres, she said: "(That) isn’t a tattoo, it’s actually a branding, he wanted to do something that was really different."

How Many Tattoos Did Pete Davidson Get Dedicated To Ariana Grande?

Ariana Grande and Pete Davidson were the definitions of a "rebound relationship," just weeks after breaking up with their longtime partners, the two were confirmed to be casually dating. Shortly after, the two stars were engaged to each other, and less than five months later, the couple had officially called it quits.

Pete Davidson often discussed how lucky he felt to be dating the popstar, during an appearance on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Falon, he said: "I feel like I won a contest, so sick, It's f------ lit, Jimmy, it's so lit. Some dude goes up to me and says, 'Yo man, you like gave me hope.' I'm like I didn't know I was that ugly". The comedian also described himself as "a lucky motherf-----"

Ariana Grande was also vocal about her love for Pete, in an appearance on Good Morning America, the pop star said that Pete "just ticks every box." She added, "It gets better every day. I'm very grateful for him. Life is beautiful."

Pete Davidson got a total of 10 tattoos dedicated to Ariana Grande during their short relationship. He got her initials "AG" tattooed on his thumb, he got the Dangerous Woman bunny mask from her album cover tattooed behind his ear. On his side, he got a portrait of "Piggy Smallz", a pet pig that they had adopted together, while on his ribs he got her last name "Grande" tattooed in cursive font.

He dedicated two back tattoos to Ariana Grande, one was a depiction of their hands intertwined, and the other was the word "always" written in her handwriting. The couple also had multiple matching tattoos. One of their first couple tattoos were cloud finger tattoos, he then copied the tattoo on the back of her neck that read "mille tendresse." Finally, they both got two matching hand tattoos, one that said "H2GKMO" (which means "honest to god knock me out,") and another one that read: "reborn."

Has Pete Davidson Removed The Tattoos That Were Dedicated To His Exes?

Pete Davidson has now covered up some of the tattoos that he got in dedication to his ex fiancé Ariana Grande, the "Grande" tattoo has now been transformed into a keyhole, the bunny ears tattoo has now been transformed into a heart, and the "mille tendresse" tattoo has clearly had removal work.

Last month, Pete Davidson who was at the beach with his potential new love interest and Bodies Bodies Bodies co-star Chase Sui Wonders, subtly confirmed to Us Weekly that he removed the tattoos he got in honor of Kim Kardashian during their relationship.

Before dating Kim Kardashian in 2022, Pete vowed to remove all of his tattoos by the age of 30, saying: "Burning them off is worse than getting them, because not only are they burning off your skin, but you’re wearing these big goggles. They said by the time I’m 30, they should all be gone. So they got, like, two more years left of this.”
