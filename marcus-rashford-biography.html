Marcus Rashford Bio (Wiki)

Who is Marcus Rashford?

Marcus Rashford is an English professional footballer. Marcus Rashford currently plays as a forward for Premier League club Manchester United and the England national team.

He is the youngest English player to score in his first senior international match.

Marcus Rashford: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

Talking about his early life, Rashford was born on 31 October 1997, in Wythenshawe, England, UK.

He is the son of English parents Robert Rashford (father) and Melanie Rashford (mother). His nationality is British and belongs to Afro-English ancestry.

Marcus Rashford has two siblings named Dane Rashford and Dwaine Maynard. Marcus spent his childhood playing football in his native place.

Marcus began his football career at Fletcher Moss Rangers at the age of five. He joined Manchester United Academy when he was seven years old. He played for the United youth team from 2005 to 2015. Marcus made it to the senior team in November 2015.

In which school did Marcus Rashford attend?

Marcus Rashford completed his education at Ashton-on-Mersey School.

Marcus Rashford: Career, Profession

Marcus played his first game for the club in the UEFA Europa League round of 32, the second leg against Midtjylland. In the game, two goals in the second half of a 5–1 win, becoming Manchester United’s youngest ever scorer in European competition.

Rashford played his first Premier League game against Arsenal, in which he scored twice and provided the assist for the other goal in a 3–2 home victory. He signed a new contract with United on 30 May 2016. In 2016, he became the runner-up in the Golden Boy award for best European player Under 21.

This player has played for team England in various age groups. He began playing for the senior team in 2016. He played his first game as a senior on May 27, 2016, in a warm-up match against Australia at the Stadium of Light.

In the game, Marcus Rashford scored the opening goal and became the youngest Englishman to score on his international debut. In 2016, he won the Jimmy Murphy Young Player of the Year award.

As a Manchester United player, Marcus Rashford has won one FA Cup, one EFL Cup, and one UEFA Europa League. He has also won an FA Community Shield.
