Biography

Paul Hollywood was born in Wallasey, England on March 1, 1966. English baker and chef who is well known as a judge on the The Great British Bake Off. He previously made an appearance on The Alan Titchmarsh Show. He worked in his father’s bakery in England before working in other bakeries.
