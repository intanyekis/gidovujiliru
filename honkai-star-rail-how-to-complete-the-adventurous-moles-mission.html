Honkai: Star Rail - How to complete The Adventurous Moles mission

If you’re wondering how to complete The Adventurous Moles in Honkai: Star Rail, look no further than this guide. We’ll rundown how to complete this mission and get to the bottom of the puzzles within. So let’s get started in this adventurous moles Honkai Star Rail guide!

Honkai Star Rail: How to start The Adventurous Moles

Firstly, you’ll need to speak with Julian after completing other quests before you can start this mission. Chances are if you’re looking at this guide you already completed this step, but if not we’ll explain how to start it here.

You’ll need to complete Hook’s Treasure and obtain Julian’s time capsule before you can start Adventurous Moles in Honkai: Star Rail. Once you have this you can find Julian outside Natasha’s clinic and speak with him.

He’ll give you a tattered old book which has some hints at some upcoming puzzles.

Honkai: Star Rail – How to complete The Adventurous Moles

First off, head to the Great Mine. Follow the navigation to get to the first page. Once there, you can pick up a miner’s lamp on a nearby box and hook it onto the nearby fence.

Head back to Julian to gain access to more Adventurous Moles puzzles in the game. The first is in Backwater Pass and requires you to pick up a page on a bench outside the cafe. You’ll then need to head upwards and flip a chair over to reveal some treasure.

The next puzzle takes place in the Silvermane Guard Restricted Zone. Travel to the Frontline Space Anchor and find three weapons that have fallen nearby. Replace them to reveal the treasure.

Finally, travel to Rivet Town and follow the navigation to the page. You’ll have to move some crates around to reveal the final treasure. Move the middle crate to a nearby shelf, and move the top crate back to where the first crate was.

Head back to Julian to inform you about your adventure and complete the final few bits of dialogue and travelling when prompted, and that’ll be the end of the Adventurous Moles mission in Honkai Star Rail!
