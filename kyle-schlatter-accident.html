Kyle Schlatter Accident: What was his Cause of death?

A man named Kyle Schlatter from Wilton, Connecticut, sadly passed away after a serious crash. The accident happened at around 10:55 p.m., just south of Simpaug Turnpike. According to the Ridgefield Police Department, Kyle was riding his motorcycle on Route 7, heading south. Unfortunately, he swerved into the northbound lane and collided with a vehicle coming from the opposite direction. Kyle’s injuries were so severe that he didn’t survive the crash. The condition of the other driver, a 48-year-old person from Bridgeport, is still unknown. The police are actively investigating the incident and are urging anyone with information or who witnessed the crash to come forward and contact Sgt. Caswell at 203-438-6531.
