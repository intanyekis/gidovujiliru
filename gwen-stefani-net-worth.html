Gwen Renée Stefani is an American recording artist with an estimated net worth of $100 million. Stefani has made the majority of here net worth from being the lead singer of the band No Doubt. She has also earned a substantial amount of money through her solo career.

She also started her own fashion line called L.A.M.B. which features many Japanese clothing styles and designs.

Gwen was born on October the 3rd, 1969 in Fullerton California. She has three siblings. Two brothers and one sister. Her brother was also in her band No Doubt as a keyboardist. When she was younger she attended both Fullerton College and California State University. She is married to another American recording artist named Gavin Rossdale. She has two kids. Her first child was born in 2006 and was named Kingston James Mcgregor Rossdale. Her second child was born in 2008 and was named Zuma Nesta Rock Rossdale.
