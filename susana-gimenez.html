Susana Giménez is an Argentinian host, businesswoman, actress, model, and vedette who is best known for hosting the self-titled television talk show which has become one of the highest-rated TV shows in Argentine television history. She has also appeared in a number of shows and movies such as Matrimonios y algo más, La piel del amor, La Mary, and Delirium.

    Susana Giménez Facts

  • She had a tough childhood as her parents separated when she was young.
  • Before making it big in the entertainment industry, Susana Giménez used to work as an executive secretary for a large factory.
  • She has often been compared to the American actress, talk show host, and philanthropist, Oprah Winfrey, as they have both successfully hosted their own talk shows.
  • Susana Giménez is friends with journalist and model, Teté Coustarot.
  • Her dog named Jazmin died in 2006.
  • She launched her own magazine, Susana, in 2008 and it covers articles on women’s issues, relationships, health, careers, celebrities, fashion, and beauty.
  • Susana Giménez has been named as one of the richest people as well as the best-paid actress in Argentina.
  • For her talk show titled Susana Gimenéz, she has won many awards like Martín Fierro Award for “Best female host” as well as Paoli Award for “Best integral show”.
  • In 2014, Susana Giménez was cast as the President of Argentina in the comedy-drama film, Delirium, which starred Ricardo Darín, Miguel Di Lemme, Ramiro Archain, and Emiliano Carrazzone.
