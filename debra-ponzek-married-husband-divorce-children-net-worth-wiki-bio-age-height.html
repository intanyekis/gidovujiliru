Who is Debra Ponzek?

Debra Ponzek is an American chef, who rose to the limelight after winning the James Beard Foundation Award for Rising Star Chef in 1992 beating her then-husband Bobby Flay. Currently, she is the owner of the Aux Delices restaurant in Connecticut.

Ponzek was born and brought up in Morristown, New Jersey. She, besides, studied engineering at Boston University, but dropped out and decided to pursue a career in cooking. In addition, she attended The Culinary Institute of Americain New York City from where she graduated in 1984.
