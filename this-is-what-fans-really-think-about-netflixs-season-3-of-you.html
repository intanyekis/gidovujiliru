This Is What Fans Really Think About Netflix's Season 3 Of 'You'

The famous psychological thriller hit series You's season 3 premiered on Netflix on October 15. The show consists of 10 episodes full of bloody events and unimaginable scenarios. Penn Badgley reprised his lead role as Joe Goldberg alongside Victoria Pedretti as Love Quinn-Goldberg, Joe's wife. Other cast members include Saffron Burrows, who plays the role of Love's mother, Tati Gabrielle as Marianne Bellamy, Shalita Grant as Sherry Conrad, Travis Van Winkle as Cary Conrad, and Dylan Arnold as Theo Engler. Greg Berlanti and Sera Gamble are the creators of the series You.

Since the release of You's third season, fans have been active on Twitter, Reddit, and other social media platforms to share their thoughts and priceless reactions about the flow of events on the show.

8 Joe And Love Are Both F***ed Up

Almost all fans of You season 3 agree that both Joe and Love are f***ed up murderers. One fan commented on Twitter that it's so funny how Joe and Love are horrified of each other, while they are both serial killers and equally violent. Another said that the couple's number of dead bodies since moving to the new neighborhood is surreal.

7 Fans Think The First Episode Is Wild Of Season 3

Just after the new season of You was released, and after fans watched the first episode of the Netflix series, they shared their thoughts about the show. Most of them considered the first episode to be wild. One person tweeted, addressing fans that they're in for a wild first episode of the third season of You. They added that they really hit the ground running to start the season.

6 They Thought Things Went Too Fast

Some fans found that the first episode of season 3 had things moving too fast. One fan shared that although events were passing quickly, they kept an open mind to what may come next. Another fan questioned whether they are enjoying the show since too much is going on and really fast. Someone else jokingly said they just started watching You season 3, and an MF was already dead.

5 Fans Are Obsessed That 'You' Dethroned 'Squid Game'

Fans of the Netflix thriller You are obsessed that the show dethroned the hit South Korean survival drama series Squid Game, which occupied the top spot as the most-watched series on Netflix for consecutive weeks. The 3rd season of You has now replaced Squid Game as the Top 1 most-watched series. One fan commented that Joe is back, and he dethroned Squid Game for the top spot on Netflix.

4 They Are Intrigued To Know More About Theo

The role of Theo Engler in the third season of the Netflix psychological thriller series You is played by American actor Dylan Arnold. Fans are wanting to know more about Dylan. The latter previously starred as Noah in the movie After. He also plays Cameron Elam in 2018's Halloween and this year's Halloween Kills. One fan wondered why Theo looks so familiar, only to later realize that he was on the cast of 2019's After.

3 Fans Didn't Expect Natalie To Be An Essential Character

The mysterious identity of Joe's neighbor was finally revealed in the third season of You as Natalie Engler. The character is played by actress Michaela McManus. Natalie is murdered in the first episode of You season 3; however, her role serves an essential purpose. One fan tweeted that they didn't expect Natalie to be one of the season's main characters.

2 They Celebrated Sherry Conrad's Win

Fans have been holding their breath since season 3 of You kicked off, fearing that Sherry and Carey Conrad will have the same fate as other neighbors. However, they felt relieved when the Goldbergs failed in their attempt to murder the Conrads. Joe and Love hated the 'Royal Couple' of Madre Linda since the day they met them, but the Goldbergs tried to maintain a good relationship with the Conrads just to blend into the neighborhood's social life.

1 Fans Are Happy That 'You' Season 3, Taught 'Anti-Vaxxers' A Lesson

Fans of You season 3 are delighted that the show sent a resounding message to anti-vaxxers and the need and urgency to get vaccinated. In episode 3 of You's new season, Henry, Love and Joe's beloved son, contracted Measles and was admitted to the hospital fighting for his life. It turns out later that Gil's daughters transmitted the disease to Henry at a birthday party. Gil apologizes to Love revealing that he doesn't believe in vaccinating his children. Love, in return, hits Gil on his head with a rolling pin and traps him in the basement of her bakery.
