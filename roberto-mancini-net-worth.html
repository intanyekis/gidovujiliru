Roberto Mancini is an Italian football manager and former international player who has an estimated net worth of $45 million.

As a player, Mancini was best known as a free-scoring attacking midfielder for his time at Sampdoria, where he played more than 550 matches and helped them win the Serie A league title, four Coppa Italia and the Cup Winners Cup, whilst being capped 36 times by Italy.
