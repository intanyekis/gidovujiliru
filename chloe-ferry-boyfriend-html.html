Chloe Ferry Might Be In It for the Long Haul with Her Boyfriend

Chloe Ferry rose to fame as part of the hit MTV series Geordie Shore. But recently, she went public with her new boyfriend, Johnny Wilbo, a professional fighter from Manchester. 

She had kept her relationship with her boyfriend low-key. However, all that changed when the reality star piled on the PDA with her lover during their fitness retreat in Norfolk. 

The couple started dating last November after meeting at the York races. 

Before Wilbo, Ferry appeared on Hot Property, a celebrity dating show where contestants choose a potential suitor by visiting their house rather than meeting and covering with them.

Speaking about the BBC reality show, she said she joined the show to find love as she felt ready to settle down. 

Chloe Ferry Opens up about Her Boyfriend

During an interview with New Magazine, the reality star revealed her big plans for the future. Despite only being together for a few months, Ferry insinuated that she was in the long haul with her boyfriend.

She revealed her desire to get engaged and walked down the aisle. The Geordie Shore star also wanted to become a mother to a large brood with many kids running around.

“I want a husband. For a long time, I was single. I’d been on my own for two years, and I thought, ‘I want to settle down now.’ I’m 26, I want a big family with 10 kids, so I feel like I need to crack on,” said the MTV star. 

But despite what she wanted, she said she needed to stop putting pressure on herself. 

Chloe Ferry and her boyfriend, Johnny Wilbo, on a date. (Source: Johnny Wilbo/Instagram)

But before having kids, Ferry wants to be financially stable for the rest of her life.

Although it sounded like her relationship seemed to progress rapidly, Ferry insisted to the Daily Mail Online that she was taking things slowly.  

She said that she enjoyed Wilbo’s company and was in a good place. 

Speaking about her new man, Ferry shared that her boyfriend was a “very genuine, down-to-earth, just lovely” person who was “always telling [her she was] gorgeous.”

Chloe Ferry Denied She Was Pregnant

Before going official with Wilbo, Ferry had to deny she was pregnant. 

The pregnancy rumors started after she shared several photos of herself in an off-white turtleneck, black mini skirt, and knee-length leather boots.

In one of the photos, she had placed her hands on her stomach as she posed for the camera. 

While in the second one, she posed on the staircase with a bag at her side. In the caption, she wrote, ‘Where baby wall or staircase or both….”

Her fans thought she was pregnant and took to Twitter to discuss the situation. 

One of them wrote, “100% pregnant. So many hints,” while another commented, “People b saying you’re pregnant with the first pic watch out.”

A third one questioned if she was pregnant. However, she quickly shut down the rumors as she posed with her alcoholic drinks.

She told fans she was 100% not pregnant, but she would love to be one day.
