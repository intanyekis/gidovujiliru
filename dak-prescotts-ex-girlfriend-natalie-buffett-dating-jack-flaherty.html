Dak Prescott's ex-girlfriend, Natalie Buffett, dating Jack Flaherty

Explore More

Model Natalie Buffett and Tigers pitcher Jack Flaherty have revealed themselves as a couple this holiday season.

Buffett posted an Instagram photo on Christmas from Longboat Key, Fla. in which she and Flaherty were kissing, with the caption, “Santa listened.”

Flaherty then re-posted the photo on his own Instagram story.

It is unclear whether or not the fateful photo was taken on Christmas Day, as Flaherty later posted photos on his stories that indicated he was sitting courtside for Celtics versus Lakers at the Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles.

Two weeks ago, Flaherty had posted a photo on Instagram with Buffett in Tulum, Mexico, with the caption being a lone rocket ship emoji.

Buffett previously dated Cowboys quarterback Dak Prescott.

The two started appearing on social media together in 2020 and were together for about two years before Page Six reported this past January that they had broken up around March of last year, but had kept the split quiet.

Prescott is currently dating Sarah Jane Ramos. The two recently announced they were having their first child together.

Last season, Flaherty was 8-9 with a 4.99 ERA and .8 WAR in 27 starts, split between the Cardinals and Orioles.

The Orioles sent minor league pitchers Zack Showalter and Drew Rom plus infielder Cesar Prieto to the Cardinals in exchange for Flaherty at the deadline.

He had previously pitched his entire big-league career for St. Louis, getting called up in 2017.

This offseason, Flaherty signed a one-year deal with the Tigers worth $14 million.

According to OutKick, Flaherty previously dated model Jenna Berman, who later was in a relationship with star 49ers pass rusher Nick Bosa, that has since dissolved.
