Gigi Hadid's Best Runway Moments: Pics

Gigi Hadid is the ultimate catwalk queen.

While Hadid’s meteoric rise to the top of the fashion industry may seem like it happened overnight, she actually started working when she was just two years old, after being discovered by Guess’ Paul Maricano. After taking a break from the modeling industry, she returned in 2012 to star in a Guess campaign. Since, she’s slayed the runway in shows including Chanel, Versace, Jacquemus and more.

During Paris Fashion Week in October 2023, Hadid was the talk of the Miu Miu spring/summer 2024 show when she went topless underneath a sparkly blazer. She paired the look with a matching skirt that exposed her bright blue underwear. Hadid topped the look off with open-toe sandals, colorful bandages on her feet, a bedhead hairstyle, eyeglasses and a fresh face.
